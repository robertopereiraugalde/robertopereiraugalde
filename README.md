## Executive Summary

I am the Founder and CEO of **NOUS**, an AI transformation company helping organizations move from AI curiosity to intelligent operations.

My work sits between strategy, software, and adoption: deciding where AI can create real leverage, building the systems that prove it, and helping teams make those systems part of the way they work.

I care about AI that is operationally useful: agents that reduce friction, workflows that preserve institutional knowledge, interfaces that clarify decisions, and automation that compounds over time.

## What I Am Building

| Area | Direction |
|---|---|
| **NOUS** | AI transformation, automation, and intelligent systems for organizations in Costa Rica and LatAm. |
| **Hermes** | Customer-facing AI support and first-contact workflows that turn inbound interest into structured context. |
| **Intelligent operations** | Internal tools, assistants, and automation layers that help teams make faster, clearer decisions. |
| **Public thinking** | Writing, experiments, and case studies on how organizations adopt useful intelligence. |

## Operating Thesis

AI will not transform organizations because someone adds a chatbot to a website.

It becomes valuable when it is designed into the operating system of the business: the way teams capture context, make decisions, serve customers, coordinate work, and learn from what has already happened.

That is the work I am interested in.

## Technical Leadership

I stay close to the implementation because the details matter. The best AI strategy is constrained by real systems: data quality, latency, security, deployment surfaces, user trust, and the everyday workflows people will actually use.

```text
Product direction       AI strategy, service design, workflows, adoption
Application layer       TypeScript, Astro, React, API routes, form systems
AI systems              OpenAI APIs, assistants, agent workflows, retrieval patterns
Infrastructure          Cloudflare Workers, edge deployment, runtime configuration
Operations              validation, observability, security, documentation, release discipline
```

## How I Think About AI Transformation

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Start with leverage** | Find the workflow where intelligence changes cost, speed, quality, or decision clarity. |
| **Build the first useful deployment** | Prove value with a specific system before scaling ambition. |
| **Keep humans in the loop** | Trust grows when people understand what the system is doing and why. |
| **Design for adoption** | The best system fails if it does not fit how the team actually works. |
| **Compound knowledge** | Every interaction should make the organization smarter, not just faster. |
